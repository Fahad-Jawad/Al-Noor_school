Who we are

The Ideal School for Your Child's Growth and Success

Opened in September 2022, we are proud to be Bury’s first Arabic language weekend school. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where children of all backgrounds and abilities can learn and develop a passion for the Arabic language.

Our comprehensive curriculum includes basic Islamic studies alongside instruction in reading, writing, and speaking Arabic. This approach provides both Arabic and non-Arabic speaking children with the ability to understand the Arabic used in Quranic prayers and equips them with valuable language skills for the future. By fostering a deep appreciation and understanding of Arabic, we aim to prepare our students for a diverse and interconnected world.

Our Vision and Mission

Empowering Future Generations with Excellence in Arabic Language Education

At Al Noor, we are dedicated to delivering unparalleled education in the Arabic language, ensuring that children of this generation not only communicate fluently in Arabic but also deeply understand the Qur'an they recite in prayer. Our mission goes beyond linguistic proficiency; we aim to instill a profound appreciation and comprehension of the sacred texts.

With the grace of Allah SWT, our devoted teaching team is committed to fostering spiritual growth and nurturing a strong sense of God-consciousness through the teachings of the Qur'an and Hadith.

At Al Noor, we celebrate diversity and inclusion by creating an Islamic learning environment that empowers and engages students. Our commitment to excellence ensures that each student is equipped with the knowledge and values needed to thrive in an ever-changing world.
