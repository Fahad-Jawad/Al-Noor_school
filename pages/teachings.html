Teaching

At Al Noor Academy, we recognize the importance of catering to diverse learning styles. Our school day is structured into two main segments: Arabic language studies and Islamic studies.

We employ a variety of engaging and interactive teaching methods to ensure that each child's educational experience is both stimulating and enjoyable. By fostering a dynamic learning environment, we aim to create lasting, positive memories for our students, making their time at Al Noor Academy both memorable and enriching.

  • Whiteboard teacher-instructed learning
  • Interactive group work learning
  • Learning through play
  • Activity-based learning
  • Nasheeds, Arabic cartoons, and Quraan recitation
  • Student class presentations
  • Board games
  • Stories and rhymes
