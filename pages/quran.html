Overview

All students are encouraged and supported in memorising the Holy Quraan with the correct Tajweed (Pronunciation).

Memorisation starts with smaller surahs and students progress through the quran if they would like to. Further to this they will also be encouraged to learn the prophetic supplications that they can implement in their day to day routine.
