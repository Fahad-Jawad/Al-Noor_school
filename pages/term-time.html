Our School Fees

Al-Noor is a non-profit organization committed to offering our services at the minimum possible cost. We require all payments to be made at the start of each chosen period, whether it is the 1st month, the 1st day of the term, or the 1st day of the school year. To streamline the payment process, we prefer that payments are set up via direct debit, though we also accept cash. Your support and adherence to these guidelines help us continue our mission of providing accessible services to our community.
